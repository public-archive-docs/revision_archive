# Solubility Rules

## Soluble in Water

- Sodium, potassium and ammonia salts
- All nitrates
- Most chlorides
- Most sulphates

## Insoluble in Water

- Sliver chloride, lead chloride
	- So - sliver chloride
	- Lame - lead chloride
- Lead sulphate, barium sulphate, calcium sulphate
	- Looking - lead sulphate
	- Bad - barium sulphate
	- Carl - calcium sulphate
